Frequently asked questions

Quick answers to the most common questions.

How do I get help with an app?
Email fdelgadosupport@proton.me with the app name in the subject line, or open the app's page above and use its "Get support" link. Please describe what happened, what you expected, and — if possible — the steps to reproduce the issue.
How long does it take to get a response?
I aim to reply to every support email within 2–3 business days. Bug reports that affect many users are prioritized.
What information should I include in a bug report?
The app name and version, your iPhone or iPad model, your iOS version, and a short description of the problem. Screenshots or screen recordings help a lot.
How do I request a refund?
Purchases and subscriptions are processed by Apple, so refunds are handled directly through them. Visit reportaproblem.apple.com, sign in with your Apple ID, and select the purchase you'd like refunded.
Can I suggest a feature?
Absolutely — feature ideas are always welcome. Send them to the same support email with the app name in the subject line.

Still need help?

Send an email and I'll get back to you as soon as I can. Every message is read personally.
