Privacy Policy

Effective Date: July 16, 2026

Vanta is a minimal notes application designed to help users create notes, lists, reminders, and checklists.

Information We Collect

Vanta does not collect, store, sell, or share personal information.

Notes and other content created in the app remain on the user’s device unless the user chooses to store or sync them through Apple services such as iCloud.

Third-Party Services

Vanta does not use advertising services, tracking technologies, analytics platforms, or third-party marketing tools.

If Apple services such as iCloud are used, Apple may process data according to Apple’s own privacy policy.

Children’s Privacy

Vanta does not knowingly collect personal information from children or any other users.

Data Security

Because Vanta does not collect personal information on its own servers, the app does not maintain a database of user information.

Users are responsible for securing their device and Apple account.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

This privacy policy may be updated if Vanta’s features or data practices change. Any updated version will be posted on this page.
